Our Mission

We believe that start-ups are a key driver of positive change and innovation in the world. To create a better future for everyone, we have to ensure that all entrepreneurs have the resources they need to succeed.

To move towards this vision we first need to challenge every assumption as to how start-ups are created and who can be a start-up founder. In turn, we need to design a system that provides every entrepreneur the resources and the community they need to succeed.

When opportunity is equally distributed, the future becomes a little better for everyone because more people have a hand in creating it.

Why Anonymity?

There’s few places where people’s ideas can come before their voice, their face, their educational pedigree and their social ties. This creates is a perpetual cycle where the people who look like they should be in a successful start-up are funded and mentored, which in turn makes them more likely to have a successful start-up.

We specifically designed Adjacent’s interface so that the Founder of an idea is anonymous. When people chat and develop a sense of trust between each other, they can choose to share personal details.

Everyone around you has an interesting idea in their head-- -sometimes we’re just so stuck seeing people as they are it’s hard to imagine where they can be.
